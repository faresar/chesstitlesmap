How to get chess title

Chess players can obtain various titles from the International Chess Federation (FIDE) through different methods. Here's a comprehensive overview of how to obtain each title:

Men chess titles

Grandmaster (GM)

  • Achieve a FIDE rating of 2500 or higher
  • Earn three GM norms in tournaments covering at least 27 games

Women chess titles

Woman Grandmaster (WGM)

  • Achieve a FIDE rating of 2300 or higher
  • Earn three WGM norms in international competition

It's important to note that for GM, IM, WGM, and WIM titles, players must achieve both the required rating and norms. For FM, CM, WFM, and WCM titles, only the rating requirement needs to be met.
