How to get chess title

Men chess titles

  • GM (Grandmaster): Rating ≥2500 + three GM norms in tournaments (27+ games)
  • IM (International Master): Rating ≥2400 + three IM norms (27+ games)
  • FM (FIDE Master): Rating ≥2300, no norms required
  • CM (Candidate Master): Rating ≥2200, no norms required

Women chess titles

  • WGM (Woman Grandmaster): Rating ≥2300 + three WGM norms
  • WIM (Woman International Master): Rating ≥2200 + three WIM norms
  • WFM (Woman FIDE Master): Rating ≥2100, no norms required
  • WCM (Woman Candidate Master): Rating ≥2000, no norms required

It's important to note that for GM, IM, WGM, and WIM titles, players must achieve both the required rating and norms. For FM, CM, WFM, and WCM titles, only the rating requirement needs to be met. Additionally, FIDE may award titles directly in certain high-level tournaments or championships, even if the usual requirements are not fully met.
